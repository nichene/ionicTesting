Amsterdam!

Amsterdam has a population of 851,373 within the city proper, 1,351,587 in the urban area,[14] and 2,410,960 in the Amsterdam metropolitan area. The city is located in the province of North Holland in the west of the country but is not its capital, which is Haarlem.

Amsterdam's name derives from Amstelredamme, indicative of the city's origin around a dam in the river Amstel. It is originated as a small fishing village in the late 12th century

As the commercial capital of the Netherlands and one of Europe's main financial centers,      Amsterdam is considered a global alpha city. The city is also the cultural capital of the country.      In 2012, Amsterdam was ranked as the second best city to live in.
